# AI Daily News Report — March 16, 2026

## LLMs & Model Releases

### 1. OpenAI Releases GPT-5.4 with Computer Use (Mar 5)
- **Source:** https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-5-4/
- Most capable OpenAI model for professional work. 1M token context window via API. First general-purpose model with native computer-use capabilities. Scored 75.0% on OSWorld-Verified, surpassing the 72.4% human baseline — first AI to demonstrably beat average human performance at using a computer. 33% fewer false claims vs GPT-5.2. Available as GPT-5.4, GPT-5.4 Thinking, and GPT-5.4 Pro.

### 2. NVIDIA Debuts Nemotron 3 Super Open Model (Mar 11)
- **Source:** https://nvidianews.nvidia.com/news/nvidia-debuts-nemotron-3-family-of-open-models
- 120B parameter open-weight model with only 12B active parameters (hybrid Mamba-Transformer MoE). 1M context window. 2.2x throughput vs GPT-OSS-120B. Designed to power complex agentic AI systems. Released alongside 10 trillion tokens of training data for developers.

### 3. Hume AI Open-Sources TADA Speech Model (Mar 14)
- **Source:** https://llm-stats.com/ai-news
- MIT-licensed speech generation model that processes text and audio in sync. 5x faster than predecessors. Produced zero hallucinations in testing. Represents a significant step forward for open-source speech AI.

### 4. ChatGPT Adds App Integrations (Mar 14)
- **Source:** https://llm-stats.com/ai-news
- ChatGPT now integrates with Spotify, Canva, Figma, Expedia, DoorDash, and Uber. Signals OpenAI's push toward an agentic ecosystem where ChatGPT acts as a unified interface across consumer apps.

### 5. Alibaba Qwen 3.5 Small Model Series (Mar 1)
- **Source:** https://www.sci-tech-today.com/news/march-2026-ai-models-avalanche/
- Four dense model variants at 0.8B, 2B, 4B, and 9B parameters. The 9B model matches GPT-OSS-120B (a model 13x its size) on benchmarks. Continues the trend of smaller models punching well above their weight.
